Xuanhuang

Society   in   GCU

a relatively large and well-known new
god organization in the Asian branch of
GCU(The GEOMANIST CLUB of the UNIVERSE).

Its overall administrative structure is a centralized system. As it has
existed since the Tang Dynasty, its departmental system is similar to the
system of Three Departments and Six Ministries in the Tang Dynasty.

Specifically, the center of power is the Cabinet, which is directly
in charge of the major departments at the lower levels. Each department
is marked by the mythical beast represented by the ancient astronomy.




Each department is briefly described below:

The Cabinet
The logo is the astrology of the Eastern Azure Dragon,
represents the center of the throne.

The central management institution of military and political centraliza
tion. It mainly consists of a president, four vice presidents, and 7-9 top
opinion members (composed of representatives in each department,
and several family leaders, and the lineup changes from time to time)
. Responsible for all internal and external instructions and decision-mak
ing on important matters. The decision will directly affect the future of
all the new gods in the Xuanhuang society, and all departments will act
according to the instructions of the Cabinet.

THE PRISON DEPARTMENT
The logo is the astrology of the Northern Black Tortoise,
represents the imprisoned shadows.

The prison holds evil gods and criminals. Gods identified as suspects
are also detained there until evidence against them is cleared. It is
divided into imprisonment, house arrest, reform, death penalty
(handed over to the Martial Department), torture (handed over to the
Punishment Department) and so on. It is a place that many new gods
are afraid of. According to legend, a lot of powerful evil gods and dan
gerous criminals of new gods are imprisoned.

THE MARTIAL DEPARTMENT
The logo is the astrology of the Western White Tiger,
represents the god of war.

The armed department of the Xuanhuang Society is also responsible
for the public security among the new gods, the intervention of armed
forces, and all the protection operations. It is a reliable combat force
defense and military intelligence enforcement department equivalent to
a police station and military defense fortress.

MARTIAL ARTS ACADEMY
Academy of the Martial Department

The armed department of the Xuanhuang Society is also responsible
for the public security among the new gods, the intervention of armed
forces, and all the protection operations. It is a reliable combat force
defense and military intelligence enforcement department equivalent to
a police station and military defense fortress.

THE INFORMATION TECH DEPARTMENT
The logo is the astrology of the Southern Vermilion Bird,
represents the whistle is sent.

The information department of the Xuanhuang Society is also responsi
ble for the public security between the new gods, the collection of
internal and external information, and all the secret documents and
Internet operations. It is a reliable information protection and document
execution department equivalent to the police station and intelligence
bureau.

INFORMATION TECH ACADEMY
Academy of the Information Tech Department

The school to train the new generation of information security manage
ment. Generally, the new gods who do not refuse the request will be
arranged here to learn the basic courses of the use of god’s power
and basic cultural knowledge, and then choose whether to stay and
continue to develop in the Information Tech Department according to
the assessment of the learning results and personal wishes. However
, the Xuanhuang Society generally emphasizes martial arts over knowl
edge-acquired course, and the study supervision of the Martial Arts
Academy is much stricter, so there are very few new gods in the Infor
mation Tech Academy, and most of them study knowledge in human
schools.

THE LABORATORY DEPARTMENT
The logo is the astrology of the Above Soaring Serpent,
represents the hidden in the fog.

The high level of the Xuanhuang Society secretly passed the bill to
research and experiment with scientific research institutions that cultivate
artificial new gods. By using the Wu family as sample, they secretly
created a number of powerful artificial new gods, some of whom
directly served in the Punishment Department after graduating
from the laboratory.

THE PUNISHMENT DEPARTMENT
The logo is the astrology of the Below Dragon Horse,
represents the punishment to break the evil.

Individual organizations formed with omnipotence, specialization, and
loyalty. The overall name is “Judges”, but each has its own ideals,
values and judgments. Most of them are anti-heroism and advocate
individuals.They have the right to lynch. Most of them help the Cabinet
to complete some extremely secret or underground missions.
